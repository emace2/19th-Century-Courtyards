Quick Setup Guide — How to Connect URP

Follow these steps in order. Each step takes under a minute.

#StepWhereNotes
1Open URP ConnectorTools → EmacE → URP ConnectorIf URP is not installed the panel offers a one-click install button.
2Select pipeline assetSection 1 — Graphics SettingsPick the asset matching your target quality (e.g. HighQuality). Use Auto-match by name to fill all quality levels automatically.
3Create Very High assetSection 1 — Quality SettingsClick Ensure Very High Pipeline Asset if the button is visible. Sets optimised shadow distance, cascades and LUT size automatically.
4Click CONNECT URPSection 1 — bottomAssigns the pipeline asset to Graphics Settings and all enabled quality levels. Activates the High level. GUIDs are regenerated if the option is checked.
5Upgrade materialsSection 2 — MaterialsAssign the materials folder and click Upgrade Materials to URP. Unity's built-in converter remaps Standard → URP/Lit shaders.
6Fix terrainSection 3 — TerrainClick Fix Terrain. Creates a URP/Terrain/Lit material per Terrain and assigns it. Save the scene afterwards.
7Connect post-processingSection 4 — Post ProcessingAssign a profile folder. Select an existing profile and click Connect, or choose a climate preset and click Generate. A Global Volume is created if the scene has none. The camera is fixed automatically (Post Processing ON, FXAA).
8Add SSAOSection 5 — Renderer FeaturesSelect the renderer asset and click Add Screen Space Ambient Occlusion. Defaults: Blue Noise, Intensity 3, Depth Normals, High quality.
9Save the sceneFile → Save (Ctrl+S)The Global Volume and camera changes are scene objects — save to keep them.
Health check: The five status dots at the top of the panel (URP · Assets · Volume · Camera PP · SSAO) turn green as each step is completed. All green = fully connected.
